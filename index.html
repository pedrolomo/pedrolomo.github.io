About

Hi! I am a 2nd year PhD researcher in Economics at the European University Institute in Florence, under the supervision of Professors Leonardo Melosi and Giancarlo Corsetti.

My research interests are in international macroeconomics, geoeconomics, industrial organization, and innovation. My current research studies how firms adapt to geopolitical risk, and its consequences for the macroeconomy.
